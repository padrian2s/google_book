Endnotes (Note 110)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 110: Tours of Duty employment model. Reid Hoffman et al. proposed the 'Tours of Duty' framework where employment is structured as a series of defined missions rather than indefinite tenure.

Key Patterns

The tours of duty concept modernizes the employment relationship for an era where lifetime employment is rare. It creates mutual accountability and clear expectations for both employer and employee.

Practical Applications

Structure roles as defined missions with clear objectives and timelines. This creates focus, enables honest career conversations, and helps both parties plan for transitions.

Endnotes (Note 98)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 98: Einstein on learning and curiosity. Letter to biographer Carl Seelig, March 11, 1952, Einstein Archives 39-013 from The Expanded Quotable Einstein (Princeton, 2000).

Key Patterns

Einstein's emphasis on curiosity and questioning over rote knowledge aligns with Google's hiring philosophy of seeking 'learning animals' who constantly seek to understand more.

Practical Applications

Value curiosity and the willingness to question assumptions in your organization. The most innovative breakthroughs come from people who ask 'why' and 'what if' persistently.

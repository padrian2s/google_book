Endnotes -- Star Trek References and Geek Culture

Commentary

Summary

This endnote (209) explains that Saurian brandy and tranya were drinks from the original Star Trek TV series. The authors hope readers who caught these references enjoyed them, and they promise this is their last Star Trek reference in the book.

Key Patterns

The Star Trek references throughout the book are not merely decorative -- they signal the deep geek culture that permeates Google and the broader technology industry. The ability to weave science fiction references into business writing reflects the culture the authors describe: one where intellectual curiosity, technical knowledge, and a sense of playfulness coexist with serious business ambition.

Practical Applications

Culture is communicated through shared references and humor. The kind of pop culture that an organization embraces reveals its values. Foster shared cultural touchstones within your teams that reinforce curiosity, creativity, and a vision of what the future could be.

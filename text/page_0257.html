Endnotes (Note 97)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 97: Grit and perseverance research. Dweck's mindset research connects to Angela Duckworth's work on 'grit' -- the combination of passion and perseverance. 'Grittier' people go further toward long-term goals across many domains.

Key Patterns

The convergence of growth mindset and grit research creates a powerful framework for talent evaluation: look for people who believe they can improve and who persist through difficulty.

Practical Applications

In hiring, look beyond raw intelligence to assess candidates' perseverance and passion for their work. Ask about projects they stuck with through significant obstacles.

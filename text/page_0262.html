Endnotes (Note 102)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 102: Academic evidence for diversity's value. Sociologist Cedric Herring found associations between racial diversity and increased sales revenue, more customers, and greater market share. Diversity also creates productive conflict that leads to better decisions.

Key Patterns

The empirical evidence for diversity's business value is strong, but the mechanism matters: diversity drives better outcomes through productive disagreement and broader perspective, not just representation.

Practical Applications

Build diverse teams deliberately and create psychological safety for disagreement. The value of diversity is realized only when different perspectives are actually heard and considered.

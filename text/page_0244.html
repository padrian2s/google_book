Commentary

Summary

Endnote 84: Y Combinator open-sourcing legal documents. Y Combinator open-sourced its term sheets and legal documents to make startup financing easier and cheaper for both founders and investors.

Key Patterns

Open-sourcing non-core assets (like legal templates) can build goodwill, reduce friction in an ecosystem, and establish your organization as a thought leader and community builder.

Practical Applications

Identify assets your organization has that could benefit your broader ecosystem if shared. Sometimes giving away tools and templates creates more value than hoarding them.

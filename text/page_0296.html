Endnotes (Note 134)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 134: Sculley's hindsight on firing Steve Jobs. In 2013, John Sculley reflected that he lacked the experience to appreciate how different 'industry-shaping' leadership is from competitive management. He suggested there could have been a different outcome.

Key Patterns

Sculley's reflections reveal the fundamental difference between operational management and visionary leadership. Organizations that confuse the two risk losing their most innovative leaders.

Practical Applications

Distinguish between managing within an established framework and leading the creation of new paradigms. The skills required are fundamentally different, and the wrong leadership style can be catastrophic.

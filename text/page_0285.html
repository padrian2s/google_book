Commentary

Summary

Endnote 123: Tufte's critique of PowerPoint. Edward Tufte, 'PowerPoint Is Evil' (Wired, September 2003).

Key Patterns

Tufte's influential critique of PowerPoint as a tool that oversimplifies complex information resonates with Google's preference for data-rich documents and analytical memos over bullet-point presentations.

Practical Applications

Challenge the default use of slide decks in your organization. For complex topics, written documents that allow nuanced argumentation often lead to better understanding and better decisions.

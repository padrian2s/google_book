Commentary

Summary

Endnote 107: Two reasons for puzzle interview questions. There are two reasons the authors like puzzle questions: First, to see if someone can deconstruct a complex problem. Second, to see if they enjoy the process.

Key Patterns

The emphasis on enjoying the process reveals a key hiring insight: people who find intellectual challenges intrinsically rewarding are more likely to thrive in an innovation-driven environment.

Practical Applications

In interviews, pay attention not just to whether candidates reach the right answer, but to how they approach the problem and whether they seem energized by the challenge itself.

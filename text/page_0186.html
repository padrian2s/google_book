Glossary

Commentary

Summary

The glossary defines key terms used throughout the book, including AdSense, AdWords, Android, API, APM (Associate Product Manager), cloud computing, Coase's law (explaining why large firms emerged but the Internet now favors outsourcing), Dory (Google's question-posting system), Excite@Home, Googlegeist (annual employee feedback survey), Google[x] (team working on self-driving cars, Glass, Project Loon), and HiPPO (Highest-Paid Person's Opinion).

Key Patterns

Google has developed its own internal vocabulary that reflects its unique culture and values. Understanding these terms is essential to understanding how the company operates.

Practical Applications

Develop and communicate a shared vocabulary within your organization that reflects your values and culture. Use consistent terminology to reinforce key concepts across the company.

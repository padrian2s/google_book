Endnote 87: Nietzsche's Zarathustra. Friedrich Nietzsche, edited by Stanley Appelbaum, Thus Spake Zarathustra (Selections) (Dover Publications, 2004).

Key Patterns

Referencing Nietzsche signals the authors' willingness to draw from philosophy, not just business literature, for management insights. The Ubermensch concept parallels the 'smart creative' archetype.

Practical Applications

Draw inspiration from diverse intellectual traditions beyond business literature. Philosophy, history, and the arts often provide deeper frameworks for thinking about leadership and innovation.

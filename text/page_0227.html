Endnotes: Facebook's Rise

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 67 cites Jessi Hempel's "How Facebook Is Taking Over Our Lives" (Fortune, February 17, 2009). This reference provides context for the book's discussion of platform competition and how social networks emerged as significant forces in the technology landscape.

Key Patterns

The authors acknowledge competitors like Facebook rather than ignoring them, reflecting the honest competitive analysis they advocate. Facebook's rise illustrates the book's themes about how platforms can rapidly scale through network effects and user engagement.

Practical Applications

Monitor emerging platforms in your industry closely -- they can grow from niche products to dominant forces faster than traditional competitive analysis would predict. Understand how social dynamics and network effects can create competitive threats that are different in kind from traditional competition.

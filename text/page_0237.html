Commentary

Summary

Endnote 77: Phone phreaking and innovation history. Phil Lapsley, Exploding the Phone: The Untold Story of the Teenagers and Outlaws Who Hacked Ma Bell (Grove/Atlantic, 2013), pages 298-99.

Key Patterns

The phone phreaking subculture, which included Steve Wozniak and Steve Jobs, represents an early example of how curious, technically skilled individuals can challenge established systems through experimentation and play.

Practical Applications

Innovation often emerges from communities of passionate tinkerers operating at the margins. Create spaces within your organization where people can experiment freely with existing systems.

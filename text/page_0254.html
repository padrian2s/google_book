Endnotes (Note 94)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 94: Henry Ford attribution uncertainty. As is usually the case with aphorisms attributed to Ford, we can't be certain it was he who said this.

Key Patterns

The authors' honest acknowledgment of attribution uncertainty demonstrates intellectual honesty. Many famous business quotes are apocryphal, and noting this builds credibility.

Practical Applications

Verify the sources of the quotes and anecdotes you use in business arguments. Intellectual honesty, even about small details, builds trust with sophisticated audiences.

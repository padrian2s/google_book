Endnotes (Note 89)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 89: Jonathan initially turning down Google. Jonathan received an offer to join Google in February 2000 and turned it down. When Eric presented another offer two years later, in February 2002, Jonathan accepted it.

Key Patterns

Even smart people make mistakes in career timing. Jonathan's initial rejection of Google is a humbling reminder that recognizing great opportunities in real-time is harder than in hindsight.

Practical Applications

When recruiting top talent, persistence matters. A 'no' today may become a 'yes' tomorrow as circumstances change. Maintain relationships with people you want to hire, even after initial rejections.

Summary

Endnote 131: Stanford d.school formal name. The school is officially called the Institute of Design at Stanford.

Key Patterns

The d.school's emphasis on design thinking has influenced how companies including Google approach problem-solving, combining empathy for users with rapid prototyping and iterative testing.

Practical Applications

Explore design thinking methodologies. The structured approach of empathize, define, ideate, prototype, and test can be applied to business challenges far beyond traditional design contexts.

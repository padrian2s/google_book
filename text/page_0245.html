Commentary

Summary

Endnote 85: Larry Page on moon shots. Steven Levy, 'Google's Larry Page on Why Moon Shots Matter' (Wired.com, January 17, 2013).

Key Patterns

Larry Page's advocacy for moon shots -- ambitious projects that aim for 10x improvement -- reflects a fundamental Google philosophy that incremental thinking leads to incremental results.

Practical Applications

Challenge your team to propose solutions that are 10x better, not just 10% better. This reframing forces entirely new approaches rather than marginal improvements to existing methods.

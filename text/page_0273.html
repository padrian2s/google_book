Commentary

Summary

Endnote 111: Tech networking and connectors. Evelyn Rusli, 'A King of Connections Is Tech's Go-To Guy' (New York Times, November 5, 2011).

Key Patterns

The tech industry's reliance on personal networks and trusted connectors highlights the importance of social capital in innovation ecosystems. Ideas flow through people, not just channels.

Practical Applications

Invest in building and maintaining your professional network. Being known as someone who connects the right people creates enormous value for your organization and career.

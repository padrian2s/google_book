Commentary

Summary

Endnote 76: Vint Cerf as smart creative. Vint Cerf, considered one of the founders of the Internet and a prime example of a smart creative, serves as Google's Chief Internet Evangelist.

Key Patterns

Having a genuine Internet pioneer as a team member reinforces Google's credibility and provides invaluable historical perspective. The 'Chief Internet Evangelist' title reflects Google's unconventional approach to roles.

Practical Applications

Seek out domain experts who embody the intersection of deep technical knowledge and creative thinking. Their presence elevates the entire organization's ambitions and standards.

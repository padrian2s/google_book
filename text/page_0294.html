Commentary

Summary

Endnote 132: Design thinking and creative confidence. Rauth et al., 'Design Thinking: An Educational Model Towards Creative Confidence' (2010).

Key Patterns

The concept of 'creative confidence' -- the belief that everyone can be creative -- aligns with Google's culture of encouraging all employees, not just designated 'creatives,' to innovate.

Practical Applications

Build creative confidence in your team by providing safe spaces to experiment and fail. Everyone has creative potential; the organizational environment determines whether it is expressed.

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 90: ASA model and talent attraction. The ASA (Attraction-Selection-Attrition) model from Benjamin Schneider shows the virtuous cycle of great people attracting other great people, building up a culture of greatness.

Key Patterns

The ASA framework explains why company culture tends to become self-reinforcing over time. Once great people join, they attract similar talent, creating a flywheel effect.

Practical Applications

Invest heavily in your first hires, as they set the cultural DNA that will either attract or repel future candidates. A-players hire A-players; B-players hire C-players.

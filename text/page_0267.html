Summary

Endnote 107: Counterfeit coin puzzle -- logical deduction. The counterfeit coin puzzle solution demonstrates systematic elimination through weighing. By dividing coins into groups and using known-good coins as controls, you can identify the counterfeit in just three weighings.

Key Patterns

This type of puzzle tests analytical thinking under constraints -- the ability to design efficient experiments that maximize information gained per step. It mirrors the scientific method applied to problem-solving.

Practical Applications

When facing complex diagnostic problems, think in terms of information theory: what single test or experiment would eliminate the most possibilities? Design your investigations to be maximally informative.

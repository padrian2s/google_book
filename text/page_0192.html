Endnotes: Google's Early Office

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 32 references the Xooglers blog (April 9, 2011), specifically a post showing a photo of Google's early office space before the Googleplex. The Xooglers blog is written by former Google employees sharing memories and stories from the company's early days.

Key Patterns

The reference to Google's pre-Googleplex days reinforces the startup mentality and scrappy beginnings that shaped the company's culture. Former employees maintaining a blog about early Google demonstrates the strong cultural bonds the company fostered.

Practical Applications

Document your organization's early days and milestones -- institutional memory helps maintain cultural continuity as you scale. Remember that today's scrappy startup workspace can become tomorrow's cultural touchstone; the environment you create matters.

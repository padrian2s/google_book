Commentary

Summary

Endnote 106: Instagram's old-school networking success. Somini Sengupta et al., 'Behind Instagram's Success, Networking the Old Way' (New York Times, April 13, 2012).

Key Patterns

Instagram's success through personal relationships rather than purely digital networking underscores that human connections remain essential even in the most digital of industries.

Practical Applications

Don't neglect in-person relationship building in favor of scalable digital approaches. The most valuable professional relationships are often built through direct personal interaction.

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 78: Mastering innovation dynamics. James M. Utterback, Mastering the Dynamics of Innovation (Harvard Business School Press, 1994), page 15.

Key Patterns

Utterback's research on how innovation evolves through phases of fluid experimentation and then consolidation provides a framework for understanding the lifecycle of disruptive technologies.

Practical Applications

Understand where your industry sits in the innovation lifecycle. During fluid phases, experiment broadly; during transitional phases, focus on execution and standardization.

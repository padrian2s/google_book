Summary

Endnote 86: Larry Page Fortune profile. Miguel Helft, 'Larry Page on Google' (Fortune, December 11, 2012).

Key Patterns

Media profiles of tech leaders provide insight into how vision and management philosophy translate into corporate culture. Page's public communications reinforce Google's innovation narrative.

Practical Applications

Leaders should use media appearances strategically to reinforce organizational values and attract like-minded talent, not just for corporate PR.

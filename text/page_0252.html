Endnotes (Note 92)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 92: Sky Map open-sourced. Sky Map was developed at Google and launched in 2009. The code was converted to open source in 2012.

Key Patterns

Google's willingness to open-source products demonstrates a commitment to the broader developer ecosystem and a belief that community stewardship can sustain products beyond their corporate lifecycle.

Practical Applications

When a product no longer fits your core strategy, consider open-sourcing it rather than killing it. This builds goodwill with developers and can keep valuable tools alive.

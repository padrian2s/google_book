Summary

Endnote 100: Tina Fey interview at Google. Tina Fey told this quote to Eric when he interviewed her onstage at Google on April 20, 2011.

Key Patterns

Google's practice of inviting creative leaders from outside tech to speak reflects a culture that values diverse perspectives and cross-pollination of ideas between industries.

Practical Applications

Bring in speakers and thought leaders from outside your industry. The most unexpected connections and insights often emerge from exposure to entirely different creative traditions.

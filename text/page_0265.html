Summary

Endnote 105: The Soul of a New Machine. Tracy Kidder, The Soul of a New Machine (Little, Brown, 1981), page 59.

Key Patterns

This Pulitzer Prize-winning book about building a minicomputer at Data General is a classic text on engineering team dynamics. Its themes of passionate engineers working against impossible deadlines resonate with startup culture.

Practical Applications

Study the history of great engineering teams. The challenges of motivating talented people to build something extraordinary are remarkably consistent across decades and technologies.

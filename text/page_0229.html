Endnotes: The Mobile Revolution

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 69 references the "Mobile Makeover" infographic (MIT Technology Review, October 22, 2013), which documents the rapid transformation of mobile technology and its impact on how people access information and services. This reference supports the book's discussion of the mobile revolution as a defining trend of the Internet Century.

Key Patterns

The mobile revolution represents one of the most significant technology transitions discussed in the book -- fundamentally changing how billions of people interact with technology. Using an infographic as a source shows the authors value diverse forms of evidence and communication.

Practical Applications

Design products and strategies with mobile-first thinking -- the majority of users now access services through mobile devices. Stay attuned to technological paradigm shifts; the transition from desktop to mobile fundamentally changed competitive dynamics across every industry.

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 107: Puzzle continuation -- harder scenario. The harder scenario occurs when the first weighing doesn't balance. The authors leave this as an exercise, demonstrating confidence in the reader's problem-solving ability.

Key Patterns

Leaving problems partially solved for others to complete is a powerful pedagogical technique. It builds engagement, confidence, and genuine understanding rather than passive consumption.

Practical Applications

When teaching or mentoring, resist the urge to provide complete solutions. Guide people to the right approach, then let them experience the satisfaction of solving the final piece themselves.

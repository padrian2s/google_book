Endnotes (Note 75)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 75: Michael Porter on specialization strategy. Larry and Sergey were unknowingly building on Michael Porter's insight that specializing on a tightly constrained group of products can achieve above-average results and enhance perceived expertise.

Key Patterns

Google's early laser focus on search -- when others were diversifying into portals -- exemplifies Porter's specialization strategy. The perceived expertise that came from being the best search engine created lasting competitive advantage.

Practical Applications

Resist the temptation to diversify too early. Build deep expertise and reputation in one area first, then expand from a position of recognized strength.

Endnotes: Avoiding Burnout

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 46 cites Marissa Mayer's "How to Avoid Burnout" (Bloomberg Businessweek, April 12, 2012). Mayer, then a Google VP and later Yahoo CEO, shared her insights on managing intense workloads while maintaining effectiveness. Her perspective adds a practical dimension to the book's discussion of work-life balance and sustainable high performance.

Key Patterns

The reference acknowledges that burnout is a real risk in high-performance cultures and requires deliberate management. Citing a Google executive's published advice shows the company's leaders practicing the openness and knowledge-sharing they preach.

Practical Applications

Develop personal strategies for sustainable high performance -- understanding your own burnout triggers is essential for long-term effectiveness. Leaders should openly discuss burnout prevention, normalizing the conversation about sustainable work practices.

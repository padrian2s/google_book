Conclusion: The Explosion of Data

Commentary

Summary

There are solid reasons for technology optimism. The first is the explosion of data and a trend toward free flow of information. From geological sensors to wearable technology like Google's smart contact lenses that track vital signs, types of data never before available are being collected at unprecedented scale. Practically limitless computing power exists to analyze this data. Infinite data and infinite computing power create an amazing playground for smart creatives to solve big problems.

Key Patterns

The combination of ubiquitous data collection and practically unlimited computing power represents a qualitative shift in problem-solving capability. Data types that were science fiction a few years ago are now routinely collected. The key constraint is no longer data or computing but human creativity in applying them.

Practical Applications

Look for opportunities where data collection was previously impossible or impractical. Combine diverse data sources to discover new insights. Invest in computing infrastructure that can handle the scale of data now available.

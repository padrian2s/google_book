Endnotes (Note 95)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 95: Carol Dweck's growth mindset research. Carol Dweck's research distinguishes between 'incremental theory' (growth mindset) and 'entity theory' (fixed mindset) of intelligence. Her popular book is Mindset: The New Psychology of Success (Random House, 2006).

Key Patterns

The growth mindset framework has become central to how Google and other tech companies think about talent development. Believing abilities can be developed leads to greater effort and resilience.

Practical Applications

Foster a growth mindset culture where learning from failure is valued over performing flawlessly. Hire people who demonstrate curiosity and resilience rather than just existing expertise.

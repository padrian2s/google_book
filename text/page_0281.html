Endnotes (Note 119)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 119: Digital transformation research. Roman Friedrich et al., 'The Next Wave of Digitization: Setting Your Direction, Building Your Capabilities' (Booz and Company, June 28, 2011).

Key Patterns

Consulting firm research on digitization provides external validation that the technology-driven transformation the authors describe is not unique to Google but is reshaping all industries.

Practical Applications

Study digital transformation across industries, not just your own. The patterns of disruption are remarkably similar, and lessons from other sectors can provide early warning and strategic insight.

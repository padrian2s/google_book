Endnotes (Note 101)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 101: Borg assimilation and diversity. We draw the line at the Borg, since being assimilated doesn't promote diversity.

Key Patterns

This Star Trek reference uses humor to make a serious point: uniformity and conformity are the enemies of innovation. True collaboration preserves individual differences while working toward shared goals.

Practical Applications

Guard against groupthink in your organization. Diversity of thought is a feature, not a bug. When everyone thinks the same way, you lose the creative friction that produces breakthroughs.

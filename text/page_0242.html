Commentary

Summary

Endnote 82: Distributed innovation principles. Karim Lakhani and Jill A. Panetta, 'The Principles of Distributed Innovation' (Innovations, Volume 2, Number 3, Summer 2007).

Key Patterns

Distributed innovation research shows that breakthroughs often come from individuals outside the core discipline, supporting the book's argument that diverse perspectives drive better solutions.

Practical Applications

When facing difficult problems, consider opening them to a broader community. Fresh perspectives from unexpected sources often yield the most creative solutions.

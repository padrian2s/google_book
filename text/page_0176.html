Conclusion: The Next Smart Creative

Commentary

Summary

Whether in crime, agriculture, pharmaceuticals, defense, energy, aerospace, or education, every pursuit will be transformed by technology. Each transformation will be driven by a small team of determined, empowered smart creatives. The authors acknowledge they are not immune to change -- there are many things they do not know, and the next generation of smart creatives sees the world differently. They are certain that for every rock star they meet in their daily work, there are dozens more working to unseat them. Somewhere in a garage or dorm room, a brave leader has gathered a team of smart creatives and may be creating a company that will render Google irrelevant.

Key Patterns

Disruption is inevitable and no business wins forever. The humility to acknowledge your own vulnerability is a strength, not a weakness. The next generation of innovators is always coming, and they see opportunities that incumbents cannot perceive.

Practical Applications

Maintain the humility to know that your current success is not permanent. Stay connected to the next generation of smart creatives and learn from their perspective. Embrace the inevitability of disruption as inspiring rather than threatening.

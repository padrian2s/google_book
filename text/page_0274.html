Commentary

Summary

Endnote 112: Tom Lehrer's dark humor. Tom Lehrer, 'We Will All Go Together When We Go,' An Evening Wasted with Tom Lehrer (Marathon Media, 2010).

Key Patterns

The reference to a Cold War-era satirist demonstrates the authors' eclectic cultural sensibility and their belief that humor -- even dark humor -- has a place in serious discourse.

Practical Applications

Maintain perspective through humor, especially in high-pressure environments. Teams that can laugh together tend to be more resilient and creative under stress.

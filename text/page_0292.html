Commentary

Summary

Endnote 130: In Search of Excellence classic. Peters and Waterman, In Search of Excellence: Lessons from America's Best-Run Companies (Harper & Row, 1982).

Key Patterns

This foundational business book identified practices of excellent companies. While some featured companies later faltered, the book established the genre of research-based management advice that this book continues.

Practical Applications

Read the classics of business literature critically. While specific company examples may age poorly, the underlying principles about culture, customer focus, and bias for action often endure.

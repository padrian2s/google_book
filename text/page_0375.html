Endnotes -- Computer Generated Imagery (CGI)

Commentary

Summary

This brief endnote (213) simply defines the acronym CGI as "Computer Generated Imagery," providing a quick reference for readers unfamiliar with the term used in the main text.

Key Patterns

The inclusion of a simple glossary-style endnote reflects the book's intent to be accessible to a broad audience, not just technology insiders. CGI represents one of many fields where computing power has fundamentally transformed a creative industry, aligning with the book's themes about technology as an enabler of human creativity and the importance of combining technical and creative skills.

Practical Applications

When communicating about technology, do not assume your audience shares your vocabulary. Providing clear, concise definitions -- as this endnote does -- ensures your message reaches the widest possible audience. Accessibility in communication is itself a form of good design.

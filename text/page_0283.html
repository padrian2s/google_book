Endnotes (Note 121)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 121: John Dewey on learning by doing. This was already a familiar saying in Dewey's day. See Hickman, The Essential Dewey, Volume 2 (Indiana University Press, 1998), page 173.

Key Patterns

Dewey's pragmatist philosophy of learning through experience deeply influences Google's approach to innovation: build prototypes, ship early, and learn from real-world feedback.

Practical Applications

Adopt an experiential learning approach in your organization. Theoretical planning has diminishing returns; real understanding comes from building, testing, and iterating in the real world.

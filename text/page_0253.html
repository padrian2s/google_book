Commentary

Summary

Endnote 93: Ray Kurzweil on exponential IT growth. Quoted in 'IT Growth and Global Change: A Conversation with Ray Kurzweil' (McKinsey Quarterly, January 2011).

Key Patterns

Kurzweil's exponential growth predictions align with Google's long-term strategic bets on AI, computing power, and data. Understanding exponential trends is essential for technology strategy.

Practical Applications

Train yourself to think exponentially rather than linearly about technology trends. What seems impossibly ambitious today may be routine in a decade given the pace of technological change.

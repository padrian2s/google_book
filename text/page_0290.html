Commentary

Summary

Endnote 128: John Wooden's leadership philosophy. John Wooden and Steve Jamison, Wooden on Leadership (McGraw-Hill, 2005), page 2.

Key Patterns

Drawing from one of basketball's greatest coaches reinforces the book's recurring theme that the best business leaders, like the best coaches, focus on preparation, principles, and people development.

Practical Applications

Study leadership across domains -- sports, military, education. The principles of building high-performing teams transcend any single industry or context.

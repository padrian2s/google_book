Table of Contents (Part 4) -- Back Matter

Commentary

Summary

This final portion of the table of contents lists the back matter sections: Acknowledgments, A Note About the Authors, Also by Eric Schmidt, Glossary, Newsletters, and Copyright. These sections round out the book with personal thanks, author credentials, and reference material.

Key Patterns

The inclusion of a Glossary reflects the book's aim to be accessible to readers outside the technology industry, while "A Note About the Authors" and "Also by Eric Schmidt" establish the credibility and context of the writers. The complete table of contents demonstrates the book's ambition to be both a comprehensive management guide and an engaging narrative.

Practical Applications

The Glossary is a valuable resource for readers who encounter unfamiliar technology or business terminology. The Acknowledgments section also provides a map of the intellectual influences and collaborators who shaped the book's ideas -- useful for further reading and research.

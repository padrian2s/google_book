Acknowledgments

Commentary

Summary

The authors thank Larry Page and Sergey Brin for their wisdom and friendship, and for building the incredible company. They note that many of the brilliant things that made Google great -- strategy, culture, emphasis on hiring excellence -- were set before either Schmidt or Rosenberg joined. They thank Ann Hiatt, Brian Thompson, Kim Cooper, Pam Shore, Scott Rubin, Meghan Casserly, Emily Wood, Rachel Whetstone, Kent Walker, Marc Ellenbogen, Dennis Woodside, Urs Holzle, and Alison Cormack, among many others.

Key Patterns

The acknowledgments reinforce the book's central theme: success comes from the collective contributions of smart creatives at every level of the organization.

Practical Applications

Recognize and appreciate the many people who contribute to an organization's success. Build strong relationships with mentors, coaches, and collaborators throughout your career.

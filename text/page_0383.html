Summary

This page contains the book's copyright information. "How Google Works" is copyright 2014 by Google, Inc., with illustrations by Nishant Choksi and cover design by Jonathan Jarvis. It was published by Grand Central Publishing, a division of Hachette Book Group, with first ebook edition in September 2014. The ISBN is 978-1-4555-8233-4.

Key Patterns

The copyright page reveals that the book is copyrighted by Google, Inc. rather than by the individual authors, suggesting the book was produced as a corporate endeavor with institutional support. The September 2014 publication date places it at a pivotal moment in tech history -- before the widespread adoption of AI, ride-sharing, and many other technologies that have since reshaped the landscape the book describes.

Practical Applications

When reading business and technology books, always note the publication date and consider how the landscape has evolved since then. The core principles in "How Google Works" -- about culture, talent, and innovation -- remain relevant, but specific examples and predictions should be evaluated against the decade of change that has occurred since 2014.

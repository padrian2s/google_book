Endnotes: Mentorship Legacy

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 57 reveals that Jonathan Rosenberg borrowed a memorable management phrase from his former boss at Apple, James Isaacs, who "once used it in a review of one of his favorite employees (or so we like to think), one Jonathan Rosenberg." This personal anecdote shows how impactful feedback and memorable phrases travel through careers.

Key Patterns

Great management phrases and feedback travel from mentor to mentee across organizations and decades. The authors' willingness to share self-deprecating personal stories adds authenticity to their leadership advice.

Practical Applications

Craft memorable, meaningful feedback for your team -- the right phrase at the right time can shape someone's career for decades. Pay attention to the best management practices you encounter throughout your career and deliberately carry them forward to your own teams.

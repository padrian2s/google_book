Commentary

Summary

Endnote 136: Biblical proverb on good news. Proverbs 25:25: 'As cold waters to a thirstie soule: so is good newes from a farre countrey.'

Key Patterns

Drawing from the Bible alongside business texts and philosophy shows the authors' intellectual range and their belief that timeless wisdom applies to modern management challenges.

Practical Applications

The best leadership principles are not new. Look to ancient wisdom traditions for insights about human nature, communication, and motivation that have stood the test of millennia.

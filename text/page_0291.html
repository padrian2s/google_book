Endnotes (Note 129)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 129: Deadlines improve decision quality. Setting deadlines helps teams pace themselves. Eisenhardt found that executive teams making fast decisions actually consider more options, not fewer.

Key Patterns

Counterintuitively, time constraints can improve decision quality by forcing focus and preventing analysis paralysis. Speed and thoroughness are not necessarily trade-offs.

Practical Applications

Set clear deadlines for decisions. Teams with deadlines tend to explore options more systematically and avoid the trap of endlessly gathering more information.

Endnotes (Note 115)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 115: Sheryl Sandberg on leadership. Sheryl Sandberg, Barnard College Commencement, May 17, 2011.

Key Patterns

Sandberg's commencement addresses have become influential texts on leadership and career development, particularly for women in technology and business.

Practical Applications

Seek out diverse leadership voices and perspectives. The most well-rounded leaders draw wisdom from a wide range of experiences and viewpoints beyond their own demographic.

Endnotes (Note 127)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 127: Conformity pressure near deadlines. Kruglanski and Webster researched how group members react to opinion deviates under time pressure, finding that deadline pressure can increase conformity and suppress dissent.

Key Patterns

Understanding how time pressure affects group decision-making is critical for leaders. Deadlines can inadvertently silence valuable dissenting voices when conformity pressure increases.

Practical Applications

When making important decisions under time pressure, explicitly create space for dissenting opinions. Designate a devil's advocate or require each team member to share concerns before voting.

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 96: Research on goals and achievement motivation. Elaine S. Elliott and Carol S. Dweck, 'Goals: An Approach to Motivation and Achievement' (Journal of Personality and Social Psychology, 1988), pages 5-12.

Key Patterns

This foundational research shows that people pursuing learning goals (mastery) outperform those pursuing performance goals (looking smart), especially when facing challenges.

Practical Applications

Frame challenges for your team as learning opportunities rather than performance tests. People take more creative risks and persist longer when they are focused on mastery rather than evaluation.

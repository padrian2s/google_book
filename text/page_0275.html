Commentary

Summary

Endnote 113: Yogi Berra's famous non-quote. Yogi Berra, The Yogi Book: I Really Didn't Say Everything I Said! (Workman Publishing, 1998), page 102.

Key Patterns

Berra's famous meta-quote about misattribution adds a layer of irony to the practice of using quotations to support business arguments. Not everything attributed to famous people was actually said by them.

Practical Applications

Use quotes and attributions carefully in professional communications. Verify sources when possible, and acknowledge uncertainty when you cannot.

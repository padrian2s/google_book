Endnotes: Facebook Reaches One Billion Users

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 68 cites Helen A. S. Popkin's "Facebook Hits One Billion Users" (NBCNews.com, October 4, 2012). This milestone -- a single platform reaching one billion users -- provides concrete evidence for the book's arguments about the unprecedented scale that technology platforms can achieve in the Internet Century.

Key Patterns

One billion users on a single platform was unprecedented in human history and exemplifies the exponential growth potential the book attributes to the Internet Century. The authors use specific milestones and statistics to ground their arguments rather than relying on abstract claims about technology's impact.

Practical Applications

Think in terms of global scale from the beginning -- in the Internet Century, the addressable market for technology products is potentially everyone on the planet. Build products and infrastructure that can handle orders-of-magnitude growth; scaling challenges are different in kind from startup challenges.

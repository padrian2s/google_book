Commentary

Summary

Endnote 83: Netflix Prize and open competition. Steve Lohr, 'Netflix Awards $1 Million Prize and Starts a New Contest' (Bits blog, New York Times, September 21, 2009).

Key Patterns

The Netflix Prize demonstrated that open competitions can solve problems that internal teams cannot. The winning solution came from a team that combined multiple approaches from different disciplines.

Practical Applications

Consider using open challenges or competitions to solve specific technical problems. The diversity of approaches generated often exceeds what any single team could produce internally.

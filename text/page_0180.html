Summary

The acknowledgments continue with thanks to Jared Cohen, Laszlo Bock, Nikesh Arora, Susan Wojcicki, Salar Kamangar, Marissa Mayer, Sundar Pichai, Lorraine Twohill, Clay Bavor, Brian Rakowski, Margo Georgiadis, and many other Googlers and former Googlers who contributed stories, feedback, and corrections. The authors specifically thank Bill Campbell as "the most gifted of all management coaches" and note that current and former Google board members always took the long view.

Key Patterns

The breadth of acknowledgments reflects Google's culture of broad contribution -- from engineers to executives, lawyers to designers, all played a role in shaping the company and the book.

Practical Applications

Value contributions from across the entire organization. Seek diverse perspectives when developing ideas and strategies. Build a culture where everyone feels ownership in the company's story.

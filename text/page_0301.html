Endnotes (Note 139)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 139 references Bill Gates' article "Bill Gates' New Rules" published in TIME magazine on April 19, 1999, which discussed the emerging rules of the digital age and how businesses must adapt to the rapid changes brought about by technology and the Internet.

Key Patterns

The authors reference thought leaders from the technology industry to bolster their arguments about how the rules of business have fundamentally changed. Gates' 1999 perspective was prescient about the transformations that would sweep through every industry, reinforcing the book's theme that traditional business approaches are inadequate for the Internet Century.

Practical Applications

Study the writings and speeches of industry pioneers who foresaw major shifts early. Their insights, even decades old, often contain timeless principles about adaptation and innovation that remain relevant as technology continues to evolve.

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 116: Google's China engineering presence. Google opened its first engineering center in China in 2005.

Key Patterns

Google's early expansion into China reflected a belief in engaging with the world's largest internet market, a decision that would later become complicated by censorship and human rights concerns.

Practical Applications

When expanding internationally, carefully weigh market opportunity against values alignment. Technical presence in a market creates commitments that may conflict with organizational values.

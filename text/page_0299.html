Endnotes (Note 137)

Commentary

Summary

Endnote 137: Culture of candor in organizations. James O'Toole and Warren Bennis, 'What's Needed Next: A Culture of Candor' (Harvard Business Review, June 2009).

Key Patterns

The call for organizational candor directly supports the book's emphasis on truth-telling, transparency, and making it safe to deliver bad news to leadership.

Practical Applications

Actively build a culture of candor by modeling transparency yourself, rewarding people who deliver difficult truths, and never punishing the messenger.

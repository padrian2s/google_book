Summary

Endnote 125: Patton quote attribution humor. Although this quote is commonly attributed to Patton, the authors couldn't find direct proof. 'But the Internet says he did, so it must be true (that's a joke).'

Key Patterns

The self-aware humor about unreliable Internet sources shows intellectual honesty and media literacy. In an era of misinformation, acknowledging uncertainty builds credibility.

Practical Applications

Model intellectual honesty by acknowledging when you cannot verify claims, even popular ones. This builds trust and encourages a culture of careful thinking and fact-checking.
